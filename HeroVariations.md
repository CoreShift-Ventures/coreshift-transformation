# Hero Header & Rotating Text - Variations

**Strategic Goal:** Sell the vision/gap, not the problem. Make it compelling enough that leaders buy into it without needing to be "sold to."

**Principles:**
- Aspirational (paint the winner's picture)
- Strategic (gap is implicit, not problem-focused)
- Simple, succinct, powerful
- Decision-maker focused

## C2 Analysis: "You Don't Need More Tools. You Need Architecture."

### **Strengths:**
✅ Strong insight (architecture vs tools)
✅ Clear differentiation
✅ Simple and memorable
✅ Framework setup (strategy → infra → execution)

### **Weaknesses:**
⚠️ Lacks aspiration/comparison
⚠️ May not create enough urgency for decision-makers
⚠️ Doesn't position them as "winners" or show the elite gap
⚠️ More explanatory than compelling

### **Decision-Maker Appeal:** ⭐⭐⭐ (3/5)
- Technical leaders: ✅ (they get "architecture")
- Business leaders: ⚠️ (needs more business outcome/aspiration)
- VP/C-Suite: ⚠️ (where's the competitive advantage? the vision?)
