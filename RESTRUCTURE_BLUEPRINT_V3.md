## Hero Video Specifications

**Duration:** 30-40 seconds
**Format:** MP4, WebM (for browser compatibility)
**Resolution:** 1920×1080 minimum (4K source preferred, downscale for web)
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (standard) or 21:9 (cinematic)
**File Size:** Optimized for web (<5MB if possible, use compression)
**Audio:** Background music (subtle, optional) + NO voiceover (keeps it universal)
**Looping:** Yes, seamless loop OR replay button after completion
**Controls:** Play/Pause, Mute/Unmute, Replay, Fullscreen (optional)

**Content:** See separate Hero Video Storyboard document for shot-by-shot breakdown

**Hosting:**
- Self-hosted on Vercel/CDN (preferred for performance)
- OR YouTube/Vimeo embed (easier but less control)

**Accessibility:**
- Captions/subtitles (if text appears in video)
- Alt text for video element
- Does not autoplay with sound (autoplay muted is okay)

## Content Tone & Voice

**Maintain Throughout:**
- ✅ Professional but approachable
- ✅ Confident without arrogance
- ✅ Clear, jargon-free language
- ✅ Benefits-focused (not feature-focused)
- ✅ Urgent but not pushy
- ✅ Transparent about pricing and process

**Avoid:**
- ❌ Overhyped AI claims ("revolutionary," "game-changing")
- ❌ Vague promises ("scale faster," without specifics)
- ❌ Enterprise jargon ("synergy," "paradigm shift")
- ❌ Fear-based messaging ("You're falling behind!")
- ❌ US-centric language or examples
