## 🎨 Design System Applied

### Colors (Matching V2):
- **Primary:** Blues (#3B82F6), Purples (#8B5CF6)
- **Gradients:** `from-purple-600 to-blue-600`, `from-blue-600 to-purple-600`
- **Success:** Green (#10B981)
- **Warning:** Amber (#F59E0B)
- **Neutral:** Grays (50-900 scale)

### Effects:
- **Glassmorphism:** `bg-white/80 backdrop-blur-sm`
- **Shadows:** `shadow-lg`, `shadow-2xl`, `shadow-purple-600/30`
- **Rounded corners:** `rounded-xl` (12px), `rounded-2xl` (16px)
- **Hover effects:** `hover:scale-105`, `hover:-translate-y-2`

### Animations (Framer Motion):
- **Fade-in on scroll:** `initial={{ opacity: 0, y: 20 }}`
- **Tab transitions:** `transition={{ duration: 0.3 }}`
- **Modal scale:** `scale-0.9 → scale-1`
- **Layout animations:** `layoutId="activeTab"`

### Typography:
- **Headlines:** 4xl-6xl, font-bold
- **Subheadlines:** xl-2xl, font-medium or semibold
- **Body:** base-lg, leading-relaxed
- **Sans-serif:** Default Next.js font stack

## 📝 Notes & Decisions

### Why Category Tabs (Not All Visible):
- ✅ Cleaner visual hierarchy
- ✅ Reduces cognitive load
- ✅ Better mobile experience
- ✅ Forces focus on one category at a time
- ✅ 3 tabs easier to navigate than 8 cards at once
- ✅ Matches screen.studio aesthetic

### Why screen.studio-style Modals:
- ✅ Beautiful, visual-first design
- ✅ Screenshot carousel is key selling point
- ✅ Shows depth without cluttering main page
- ✅ Auto-scroll creates "motion" and engagement
- ✅ Lightbox for detailed viewing
- ✅ Professional, modern UX

### Why Process-First (Not Industry-First):
- ✅ Universal appeal (any industry can relate)
- ✅ Simplifies GTM (don't need industry expertise upfront)
- ✅ Product positioning (templates, not consulting)
- ✅ Scalable (build once, sell many times)
- ✅ Easier to maintain (one set of mockups)
- ✅ Industry adaptation happens in Blueprint Sprint
